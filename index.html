Buy & Borrow Mobile App

Buy & Borrow is a mobile application that allows users to search for commonly needed items that are available in their local community, either to purchase or to borrow from another person. The goal of this project was to give users fast and efficient access to information of where to find needed items, and also to drive a sense of community post-pandemic.

Non-for-Profit Redesign

Edgar's Mission is an Australian non-for-profit organisation that provides care to over 400 rescued farm animals. The goal of this project was to improve the digital presence of the organisation through a website redesign, resulting in higher donation, volunteering, and visitation rates.

ATO Redesign

The Australian Taxation Office provides service to users looking for key information for thier businesses, or their own finances, and is a hub for all resources to do with your tax return. The goal of this project was to redefine the Information Architecture of the ATO website, giving it a visual and stylistic overhaul in the process.

Impact Training & Volunteering App

What started as a redesign of the 300 Blankets website, turned into an idea to kickstart people's volunteering work through education and connections with others. The goal of this project was to aid users who were looking to volunteer for causes they believe in, but were unsure what the best ways to help are, and where to find information.

Skills

User Research

To approach user research I begin with a proto persona. I want to understand the user the best way I can. Beginning with a leading statement, I tend to form an initial hypothesis, and use it alongside my persona to craft the best possible research path that will solve user needs. Initial work is carried outthrough Miro boards, and pen and paper.

Qualitative Interviews

Qualitative interviews are so important to the UX phase, and coming with the right questions in crucial. I like to ask leading questions where I can, and really allow the interviewee time to think and describe why their answer is important to their life. Creating a script that targets my persona is also very crucial, and I work to make sure everything I found out during an interview will be valuable in either validating, or in-validating my initial thoughts.

User Empathy

When I empathise with users, I try to see a problem from their perspective. Drawing from problem statements and other key insights, helps to understand not only if a user needs a certain problem solved, but why they need it solved. I like to spend a lot of time in the empathy phase of UX design, so the end result can be as tailored to the user as possible.
